## Full description (≤ 4000 chars)
```
ThinkThru helps a community think through a decision together — and remember why
it was made.

Plant a seed (a question or a choice you're facing). Invite the people it
affects. Everyone explores it across a few simple dimensions, shares their view,
and weighs in on what matters. When the group is ready, the seed "blooms" into a
clear decision that stays — with the reasoning, the trade-offs, and the people
who shaped it preserved alongside it.

What makes ThinkThru different:

• The Quorum — instead of a loud majority, ThinkThru reads how much each person
  carries on a decision (the effort, the stakes, the judgement, the heart) and
  turns that into a fair weight. Quiet voices with real stake aren't drowned out.

• A living memory — every decision becomes part of your community's Sacred Tree,
  so months later nobody has to ask "wait, why did we choose this?"

• Helpful AI — ask Claude or ChatGPT to summarise a long discussion, mediate a
  disagreement, or pull in current facts — on demand, never in your way.

• Gentle notifications — a nudge when your seed blooms, when you're mentioned, or
  when your point lands. One calm daily digest for everything else.

Good for families making a big call, teams making a decision, friends planning
something together, or any group that wants to decide well — and remember how.
```
